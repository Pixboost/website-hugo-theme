Web Performance Newsletter

Here @ Pixboost we obsessed with making the Web faster. We believe that everyone would benefit from the enhanced performance:

  • Users. No need to wait until the page is loaded to browse the content
  • Owners. By providing better User eXperience, you are building a long-lasting relationship with your users.
  • Our Planet. How often do you buy a new phone? Do you know what happens with your old phone when you throw it away? If we can create high-performance web sites that won't fry our phone, then we can use the same device for longer without need to upgrade.

We are going through tons of fascinating articles when developing the service. Why not share? That's why we created this newsletter. You'll find all the publications that we read and found ourselves fascinated about in our regular email. And no, we won't send you any promo links cause that's beyond web developer honour.
